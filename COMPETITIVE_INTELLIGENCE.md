## Executive Summary

This document catalogs the strongest-performing pages from competitors across four property management verticals: residential, HOA/community association, commercial, and multifamily. Research was conducted using Ahrefs domain-level metrics, top-page traffic analysis, site structure mapping, and strategic page evaluation.

### Key Findings

1. **Bay Management Group** (DR 63 | 40,800 monthly organic traffic | 12,759 keywords) is the strongest direct residential PM competitor in the DC/MD/VA market. Their content strategy — aggressive blog production targeting tenant and landlord questions — drives the bulk of their traffic.

2. **FirstService Residential** (DR 72 | 115,291 monthly organic traffic | 11,449 keywords) is the dominant HOA management brand nationally. Their state-specific legal content strategy (squatter's rights guides by state, HOA explainer content) is a masterclass in topical authority building.

3. **Bozzuto** (DR 71 | 59,819 monthly organic traffic | 5,827 keywords) dominates multifamily visibility through extensive property listing pages with strong geo-targeting and branded search capture.

4. **Atlas Lane** (DR 20 | 2,477 monthly organic traffic) punches above its weight by building neighborhood-level geo pages (50+ DC neighborhoods) and DC-specific blog content. This is the most replicable model for Gordon James.

5. **Nomadic Real Estate** (DR 35 | 2,808 monthly organic traffic) has built strong service-intent content and is the closest direct competitor in terms of DC market overlap and target audience.

### Gordon James Realty Current Position
- **DR 15 | 493 monthly organic traffic | 181 keywords**
- Strong blog foundation (150 articles optimized) but lacking geo pages, service pages, and competitive content depth
- Biggest gaps: location/neighborhood pages, service-line landing pages, head-term educational content

### Pattern 2: AI SEO Content Cluster

1. **Definition / glossary page** ("What is an HOA?") — captures head-term queries and AI definition answers

3. **Comparison / decision page** ("Self-Managed vs. Professional HOA Management") — captures decision queries
4. **Core service page** ("HOA Management in Washington DC") — captures conversion intent

Internal linking between all four creates a topical cluster that signals authority to both traditional search and AI systems.

- Residential PM: definition + DC landlord-tenant law + self-manage vs. hire PM + DC PM service page

- Commercial PM: definition + DC BEPS guide + PM costs/comparison + commercial PM service page
