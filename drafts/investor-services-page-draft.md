### Increase Rent Performance and Reduce Vacancy

Vacancy is the most expensive cost an investor faces. We begin every engagement with a detailed market analysis, pricing the property competitively based on current conditions, amenities, and property condition. We market across 13+ platforms with professional photography, respond to prospective tenants within 15 minutes, and have placed tenants in as few as 5 days for well-priced properties.

Our average time on market is 19 days. We contact tenants 60 days before the end of each lease to negotiate renewals, keeping strong tenants in place and reducing turnover costs that routinely run thousands of dollars in this market.

### Improve Owner Visibility and Monthly Reporting

Investors need more than a monthly check. Through our secured online owner portal — built on the Salesforce platform — you get 24/7 access to monthly financial statements, maintenance activity, invoices, lease documents, and expense records. All contractor invoices and photographs are uploaded directly to your portal. During tax season, your records are organized and ready for Schedule E reporting.

### Protect Compliance Across DC, Virginia & Maryland

DC, Virginia, and Maryland each have distinct landlord-tenant laws, licensing requirements, and regulatory obligations. DC alone requires a Basic Business License, passed housing inspections, rent control filings, and compliance with the Tenant Opportunity to Purchase Act. Virginia and Maryland carry their own licensing and security deposit rules.

We stay current on the regulatory landscape across all three jurisdictions so your property is managed in full compliance — and you avoid the fines, delays, and legal exposure that come from missed requirements.

### Reduce Operational Drag With Maintenance and Vendor Coordination

We handle all maintenance coordination through our vetted vendor network and in-house maintenance staff. Tenants submit requests online at any time. We coordinate repairs, track completion, and upload invoices and documentation to your portal. Our 24/7 emergency response team means issues are addressed immediately — whether you are across town or across the world.

### Support Renewals, Retention & Property Readiness

Tenant turnover is expensive. We manage lease renewals proactively, conduct detailed move-in and move-out inspections using custom inspection software, and coordinate all turnover maintenance so properties are move-in ready — even on a 24-hour window. We handle the documentation, security deposit reconciliation, and transition logistics from start to finish.

### Tenant Screening That Protects Your Investment

Through our in-depth screening process using TransUnion, we check credit histories, conduct criminal background checks, perform employment verifications, and consult prior landlord references. Our screening process is fully compliant with DC, Virginia, and Maryland fair housing laws, including DC's Fair Criminal Record Screening Amendment Act and source-of-income protections.

We have placed tenants with zero successful evictions filed against them. Rigorous screening protects your income stream and avoids the months of lost rent and legal costs that follow a poor placement — especially in DC, where eviction proceedings can take six to twelve months.

## Section 6 — Why Local Execution Matters in DC, VA & MD

**Section heading:** Why Local Execution Matters for Investor Success

Investor returns in the DC metro area depend on strong local execution — not just good intentions from a management company.

**Pricing judgment:** Rental pricing in DC, Arlington, Fairfax, Alexandria, Bethesda, and Silver Spring varies meaningfully by neighborhood, property type, and season. Mispricing a property by even a small margin can cost weeks of vacancy or thousands in lost annual rent.

**Fast leasing response:** Prospective tenants in this market move quickly. We respond within 15 minutes and schedule showings at times that work for them. Speed matters when good tenants are evaluating multiple properties.

**Tenant quality:** Screening standards need to reflect the regulatory environment. DC's Fair Criminal Record Screening Amendment Act, source-of-income protections in DC, Montgomery County, Fairfax County, Arlington County, and Alexandria, and jurisdiction-specific security deposit rules all affect how screening is conducted. A screening error can create compliance exposure before a lease is even signed.

**Maintenance coordination:** Vendors who know DC rowhouses, Northern Virginia condos, and Maryland single-family homes respond faster and solve problems more effectively than national dispatch services. Our vetted vendor network and in-house maintenance staff operate locally and respond around the clock.

**Local compliance awareness:** DC, Virginia, and Maryland each carry different licensing, inspection, security deposit, and tenant notification requirements. Managing across jurisdictions without understanding those differences creates risk that most investors do not want to carry.

**Different operating realities:** A condo in Arlington, a rowhouse in DC, and a single-family home in Bethesda each present different leasing dynamics, maintenance patterns, and regulatory obligations. Local management means understanding those differences and managing accordingly.

### How much does investor property management cost with Gordon James Realty?

Our management fee ranges from 7% to 8.5% of monthly rent collected — and you only pay when your unit is occupied. There is no fee during vacancy and no upfront costs. For our full leasing package, which includes market analysis, professional photography, advertising across 13+ platforms, tenant screening, and lease administration, the fee is 90% of one month's rent. We also offer a partial leasing package for $400 if you have already identified prospective tenants. Contact us for a custom quote based on your property or portfolio.

### What types of investment properties does Gordon James manage?

We manage single-family homes, condominiums, and multi-family properties throughout Washington DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland — including Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, Tysons, Bethesda, Potomac, and Silver Spring. We serve individual rental property owners, repeat investors, overseas and military owners, and small portfolio owners with mixed residential holdings.

### Can Gordon James manage my property if I live overseas or out of state?

Yes. We manage properties for owners stationed overseas, serving in the military or foreign service, and living in other states. Our 24/7 owner portal provides real-time access to financial statements, maintenance activity, lease documents, and property status from anywhere. We handle all local operations — leasing, maintenance, compliance, inspections, and tenant communication — so distance does not affect how your property is managed.

### How do you reduce vacancy for investment properties?

Our average time on market is 19 days. We begin with a detailed rental analysis to price your property competitively.
